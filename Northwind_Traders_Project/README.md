# 📦 Northwind Traders Analytics

## 🧩 Project Overview
This project analyzes business operations of Northwind Traders, a global import-export company. 
The focus is on evaluating sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency using SQL and Power BI.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Assess overall revenue performance and growth trends  
- Identify top customers and high-performing products  
- Analyze sales representative performance  
- Evaluate operational efficiency and delivery performance  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- MySQL (Data Extraction & Querying)  
- Power BI (Data Modeling & Visualization)  

---

## 🧮 Data Processing
Data was extracted and transformed using SQL:
- Created structured tables (Orders, Customers, Products, Employees)  
- Developed 50 analytical queries across multiple levels  
- Generated datasets for reporting (CSV exports)  
- Ensured relational integrity between all tables  

---

## 📊 Analysis & Features
- KPI Measures:
  - Total Revenue  
  - Total Orders  
  - Average Order Value ($1.4K)  
  - Revenue Growth (%)  
  - On-Time Delivery Rate  

- Advanced analytics:
  - Customer segmentation  
  - Monthly revenue trends  
  - Product category performance  
  - Geographic sales distribution  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

### 1. Sales Executive Overview

### 2. Customer & Sales Rep Performance
- Top 10 customers by revenue  
- Sales contribution by employees

### 3. Product & Inventory Intelligence
- Revenue by category  
- Product demand distribution  
- Inventory performance insights  

### 4. Operational & Logistics Analysis
- Delivery performance metrics  
- Shipping efficiency trends  
- Order fulfillment analysis  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Revenue is concentrated among a small group of high-value customers  
- Certain product categories consistently drive the majority of sales  
- Average order value indicates strong bulk purchasing behavior  
- Delivery efficiency directly impacts customer satisfaction and retention  

---

## 💼 Business Impact
This analysis helps the organization to:
- Improve revenue generation strategies  
- Strengthen customer retention  
- Optimize product offerings  
- Enhance operational efficiency
