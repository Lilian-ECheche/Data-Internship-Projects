# 📊 HR Attrition Analytics

## 🧩 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition patterns within an organization using structured data analysis techniques. 
The objective is to uncover key drivers of employee turnover and provide actionable insights to support better workforce retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify factors contributing to employee attrition  
- Analyze trends across departments, roles, and demographics  
- Evaluate the impact of compensation, satisfaction, and overtime  
- Develop insights to support HR decision-making  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel (Power Query, Formulas, PivotTables)  
- Tableau Public (Dashboard Development)  

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation
The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Power Query:
- Removed constant and irrelevant columns  
- Converted coded fields into readable categories  
- Created derived fields (Age Band, Income Band, Tenure Band)  
- Developed key indicators such as Attrition Flag and Risk Scores  
- Ensured logical consistency across all variables  

---

## 📊 Analysis & Features
- 15 Pivot Tables analyzing attrition across multiple dimensions  
- 5 Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering  
- KPI calculations including:
- Attrition Rate  
- Average Age  
- Average Income  
- Job Satisfaction Levels  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights
The Tableau dashboards provide insights across three key areas:

### 1. HR Executive Summary
- Overall attrition rate and employee distribution  
- Department-level attrition trends  
- Overtime impact on employee turnover  

### 2. Compensation & Performance
- Salary distribution across job roles  
- Relationship between income and attrition  
- Training and performance analysis  

### 3. Attrition Risk Intelligence
- High-risk employee segments  
- Promotion stagnation patterns  
- Satisfaction vs attrition heatmap  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Employees working overtime show significantly higher attrition rates  
- Lower job satisfaction strongly correlates with employee exits  
- Certain departments consistently experience higher turnover  
- Compensation imbalance contributes to attrition risk in key roles

---

## 💼 Business Impact
The insights from this analysis enable organizations to:
- Improve employee retention strategies  
- Optimize compensation structures  
- Enhance workforce satisfaction  
- Reduce operational costs associated with high turnover
